Reference for known protocol data:
- Idle frame: FE 06 70 XX 00 06 70 00 (8 bytes, byte 3 varies with temp)
- Confirmed: 0x30="1", 0x70="7", 0x00=blank
- FE byte: status indicator (present during idle, absent when lights on)
- Sub-frame model: bytes [0-3] and [4-7] may represent two display zones
- GS510SZ reference encoding: bit7=dp, bits6-0 = segments a-g
</context>
