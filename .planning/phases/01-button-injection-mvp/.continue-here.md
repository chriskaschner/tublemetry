<decisions_made>

- Phantom press root cause: T-splitter stubs all 8 pins; >12" unterminated stub on Orange pair (Pin 1 +5V / Pin 2 Temp Up) acts as antenna, couples into button line
- Fix: stub cable with conductors ONLY at pins 4/5/6 -- button line pins have zero stub at T-splitter third port
- Use Waveshare USB RS-485 adapter for capture (not ESP32) -- direct serial port, no firmware needed
- macOS port naming: /dev/cu.usbserial-XXXX (not COM3, not /dev/ttyUSB0)
- CH340 driver may be needed on macOS -- check /dev/cu.* after plugging in before assuming it works
- OEM panel cable (factory) uses non-standard colors, same pin orientation as T568B:
  Pin 1 Brown, Pin 2 Blue, Pin 3 Yellow, Pin 4 Green, Pin 5 Red, Pin 6 Black, Pin 7 Orange, Pin 8 Grey
</decisions_made>
